About Kalna Polytechnic

This polytechnic is affiliated to the-WBSCTE,and is recognised by the AICTE,new delhi,this polytechnic offers Diploma courses in computer science and technology(DCST), electronics & telecommunications(DETCE) and civil engineering (DCE).it It also has on site hostels for girls only.
